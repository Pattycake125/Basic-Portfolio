About Me

    With a AAS degree Mechanical Technology degree and a BA in Business Administration along with a love of technical stuff, I have been able to work in industry for nearly 30 years connecting whatever equipment has a communications port. Doing this often means creating my own connection software and constructing communication cables.
    Doing this work, developed my love for coding. Investigating all areas of my company, I would find bottle necks where shared communication could help speed them up.
    With a natural analytical mind, and high organization skills, I developed integrated document control systems that used shareware software for cost reduction, and web control making ISO certification a breeze.
